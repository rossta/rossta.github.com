Progressive Web Apps on Rails

Use this little gem for integrating Service Workers with Rails

Star me on Speaker Deck!

At Arlington Ruby, I gave a very brief (inadequate) introduction to Progressive Web Apps and Service Worker and how you can incorporate these concepts into your Rails app with my gem, serviceworker-rails.

Be sure to check out my blog post where I go into more depth on how to add a simple Service Worker to your Rails app.
