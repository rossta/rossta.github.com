Enumerator - Enumerable's Ugly Cousin

Talk at Ancient City Ruby Conf, 2016

The Enumerator class in the Ruby standard library is a powerful concept that doesn't seem to get enough attention amongst Rubyists. This presentation takes a close look at the fundamental ideas on which Enumerator is built along with some practical takeaways for your daily work.
